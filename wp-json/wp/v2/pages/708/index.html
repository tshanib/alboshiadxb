Al Boshia is one of the major leading Fire & Safety company and actively engaged in this line of activities in the Emirate of Dubai since 2005.<\/span><\/p>

Al Boshia has grown to prove that it is one of the most trusted companies in Fire & Safety Services providing the best solutions for supply, installation, testing, commissioning, maintenance, annual maintenance contracts and after sale services for all kind of Fire & Safety Systems and Personal Safety Products.<\/span><\/p>

Al Boshia is certified from Dubai Civil Defence & Abu Dhabi Civil Defense (ADCB) and working under National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) standards, and ISO 9001: 2015 certified for installation and repair of fire alarm system , firefighting system, fire extinguishing system, clean agent fire suppression systems.<\/span><\/p>

We have a team of well qualified dedicated and professionally trained Engineers and Technicians with high level of efficiency in service and to solve all requirements with in the fire and safety business working under experienced management.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

At Al Boshia services are done as per Dubai Civil defense regulations. We use DCD approved products only. Our after sale services are one of the best services that can be provided in the field of fire and safety.
